Multimedia content plays a vital role in delivering engaging experiences.

Using the <video> tag, I can embed local video files directly into a webpage, giving users control over playback.
For example, a product demo video or a tutorial can be seamlessly integrated.

Similarly, with the <iframe> tag, I can embed videos from platforms like YouTube or Vimeo, allowing for streaming external content without hosting it ourselves.

Additionally, the <audio> tag lets me embed audio recordings such as podcasts or music tracks, making it easy to share rich audio experiences.

Together, these tools make multimedia integration both flexible and user-friendly.
